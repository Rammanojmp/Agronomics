Upload an Image for Crop Progress Tracking

Welcome to the crop progress tracking tool! By uploading a monthly image of your crops, you can monitor their growth and overall health over time. This helps you make informed decisions about crop care and schedule timely interventions.

How it Works

Our system allows you to regularly upload images of your crop fields. Over time, you can view a history of these images to track progress, identify improvements, and detect any emerging issues.

Steps to Upload Your Crop Progress Image

  1. Click on the "Choose File" button below.
  2. Select the image of your crop field that you want to record for progress tracking.
  3. Click "Upload Image" to submit the file.
  4. Your image will be added to your progress history, allowing you to compare it with previous uploads.

Why Track Crop Progress?

Regularly tracking your crop images helps you monitor growth patterns, notice early signs of stress or nutrient deficiency, and plan for interventions such as fertilization or pest management.

Supported File Formats

For best results, please upload images in the following formats:

  • JPG
  • PNG
  • JPEG
